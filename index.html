How to apply for your visa

A clear, step-by-step guide that explains the visa application process for international students coming to Australia.

Quick summary

Prepare documents, check eligibility, apply online via ImmiAccount, then wait for the outcome. You may need health checks and evidence of financial capacity.

Step 1

Check your visa type

Confirm which student visa subclass suits your course and stay length.

Step 2

Gather documents

Passports, COE (Confirmation of Enrolment), English evidence, financials, health insurance.

Step 3

Apply online

Create an ImmiAccount, complete application, pay fees and submit your documents.

Before you apply

  • Ensure your COE matches your application.
  • Check health and character requirements.
  • Have Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) details ready.

Processing times & outcomes

Processing times vary. After decision, you'll receive a visa grant number and conditions. Keep copies of correspondence.

Need help?

If you're unsure, contact the international student support at your chosen institution or seek registered migration advice.

Checklist
  1. Valid passport
  2. Confirmation of Enrolment (COE)
  3. Proof of funds
  4. OSHC details
